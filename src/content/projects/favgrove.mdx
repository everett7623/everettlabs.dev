## What it solves

Browser bookmark managers become unwieldy as collections grow. FavGrove turns bookmarks into a full-screen workspace for search, review, and safe cleanup without sending data to a server.

## Who it is for

People with thousands of bookmarks who want to organize, deduplicate, and back them up locally.

## Why it exists

Cloud bookmark tools often require accounts and uploads. FavGrove keeps processing on the user's device, with no developer-controlled analytics or cloud sync.

## Key capabilities

- Full-screen bookmark workspace
- Duplicate detection and folder management
- Link review, tags, and virtualized lists
- Local backups, import, and export
- Fast search across large collections
- Chrome and Edge support via Manifest V3

## Engineering notes

FavGrove is built with React, TypeScript, Vite, Manifest V3, CRXJS, Zustand, Tailwind CSS, Vitest, and Playwright. Everything stays local by default.
