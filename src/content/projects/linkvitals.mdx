## What it solves

WordPress sites accumulate broken links, redirects, and orphaned pages over time. LinkVitals scans content without adding front-end tracking, then reports what needs attention.

## Who it is for

WordPress site owners, content editors, and SEO-focused teams who need a lightweight, privacy-aware audit tool.

## Why it exists

Existing link checkers often inject beacons or analytics. LinkVitals runs server-side scans, keeps data local, and surfaces actionable results.

## Key capabilities

- Broken link, redirect, timeout, SSL, and DNS failure detection
- Invalid anchor and orphaned-page identification
- External-link SEO risk analysis
- Scheduled scans with exportable reports
- Repair history with rollback safeguards
- Optional AI-assisted replacement suggestions

## Engineering notes

LinkVitals is a WordPress plugin written in PHP. It performs scans without loading tracking scripts on the public site, keeping visitor privacy intact.
