## What it solves

International operations and logistics require repetitive calculations and lookups across many tools. GloboKit brings them into one fast, modern interface.

## Who it is for

Operators, importers, exporters, and small teams who need quick calculations without switching between spreadsheets and websites.

## Why it exists

Most specialized logistics tools are either bloated enterprise suites or single-purpose calculators. GloboKit aims for the middle ground: useful, fast, and self-contained.

## Key capabilities

- Quotation and profit calculations
- Landed cost and shipping estimates
- Pallet and container planning
- Barcode generation and text utilities
- Country data, time zones, and holiday lookups

## Engineering notes

GloboKit is built with Next.js, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, shadcn/ui, Radix UI, and Lucide. It runs entirely in the browser where possible.
